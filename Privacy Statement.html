PRIVACY STATEMENT & DISCLAIMER

This privacy statement informs about how your personal data is collected, used and processed by claudiawinkler.net (“the provider”).

Access Data

The provider and his webspace provider collect data on every visit to the webpage (so called server log files). These include: the name of the webpage visited, the date, the time, the quantity of the data transferred, the browser type and version, the operating system of the user, the referring URL (the page visited prior), the IP address and the requesting provider. The provider only uses this data for statistic purposes in the interest of his business, the safety and the optimization of his page. The provider retains the right to use this data to investigate illegal use of his services. Handling of personal data Personal data is information that helps to individualize a person. This information includes the name, email address and telephone number but also information like hobbies, memberships or webpages visited. The provider only collects, uses or forwards such personal data in cases where this is not against the law or in cases where the user agrees to the collection of his/her data. Communication with the provider

In case of communication with the provider (e.g. via the contact form or email) the data of the user will be collected for purposes of processing the query or for possible follow up questions by the user. Inclusion of services and content of third parties It may occur that content of third parties is included in our online content, i.e. videos from YouTube, maps from Google-Maps, RSS-Feeds or graphs from other websites. This means that the providers of these services (in the following “third party providers”) collect the IP-address of users to be able to send their content to the user’s browser. The IP-address is a necessity for the display of such content. We make every effort to only use the content of providers which use the collected IP-addresses exclusively for purposes of the delivery of content. However, we do not have any influence in case a third party provider collects the IP-address for other, e.g. statistical, purposes.

Cookies

Cookies are small data which enable to save specific device-related data on the user’s access device (i.e. PC, smartphone, etc.). These make websites more user-friendly and are therefore in the user’s interests (e.g. storage of login-data). They are also used to collect statistical data on the use of the website and to analyze the use of a website in order to improve the content. The users can decide on the use of the cookies. Most browsers include an option that limits or entirely prevents the storage of cookies. Please note, however, that the use of website and particularly the ease of use is substantially limited without the use of cookies. You can administer several online-cookies of corporations via the U.S. website http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ or the EU-website http://www.youronlinechoices.com/uk/your-ad-choices/ Withdrawal, amendments, user rights and updates The user has the right to be informed about all personal data stored about him/her without charge. In addition, the user has the right to request the correction of incorrect data as well as to request the blocking or deletion of personal data, provided that there is no legal obligation of the provider to preserve this data.
